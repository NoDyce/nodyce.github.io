Job Placement

As a job placement specialist, we will find and place effective job candidates, with or without disabilities within the job field of their choice, or what we decide together to be suitable employment for the client. We will match job requirements with possible candidates, conducting extensive research based on the position needs, and interacting with employers and prospective candidates. We will provide training opportunities as required and give detailed guidance to prospective employees regarding potential benefits and job responsibilities. We will contact possible employer's using a variety of methods such as via telephone, by email, or in person. We will follow up with interviews and technical testing, as well as verify references and perform eligibility checks.

Job Coach

We specialize in assisting individuals with disabilities to learn and accurately carry out job duties. We will provide one-on-one training tailored to the needs of the employee. We will do a job analysis to identify the job duties, followed by developing a specific plan as to how we can best train the employee to work more and more on his/her own until completely self-sufficient and able to perform job duties accurately and effectively without assistance.
