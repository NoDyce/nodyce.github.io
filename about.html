NoDyce Global LLC is a disabled veteran, minority owned and operated logistic company with an office in El Paso, Texas. We have a vast array of sources in the UK, Middle East, Asia, and the United States that help us to provide the best possible price and solutions for your logistical needs.

Our objective is to provide a complete integrated logistic service and solution to our customers by expanding our resources, providing fair prices, quality equipment and a service unmatched by any competitor.

Potential Clients

We are currently marketing our services to all government, state, local agencies and prime contractors looking for a loyal and reliable small business that will save them a tremendous amount of money by doing the job with the smallest budget possible with quality work still being our main objective.

Employees

We have experienced and well trained professionals who help to meet all of our clients' needs. We have a vast pool of manpower resources from North America, South America, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East to meet demand for personnel.
